01/02/2020 – 27/06/2020 – Siliana, Tunisia

Civil engineering (End of studies internship)

Ministry of Equipment, Housing and Territorial Development For my end of studies internship I got an internship within the Ministry of Equipment, Housing and Territorial Development in Tunisia, the task was to build a beam bridge in Bourada of 59m length and 7m width, and reinforcement of a 20k m road on both sides. Unlike to the other two internships, for this one I took part of all the phases of design of the project. The most challenging part was doing the math and calculating the different solicitations in all layouts, the resistance of the bridge against the overloads of different kinds of vehicles and finally calculating the coefficient of transverse distribution CTD following the abbacies of professor Michel Massonnet. Since the bridge is over a river, I also got to do the hydraulic and the hydrological studies using a software called Argic, that we also used to do the watershed delimitation. I finally wrote a 230 pages report about the project and defended my thesis in front of a Jury and graduated with a "Very good" honors and a 17/20 mark. Today Boarada bridge help more than 300 driver reach their destination safely.
